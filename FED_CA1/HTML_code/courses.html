Singapore Polytechnic's courses on computing

First, a little about my current experience in Singapore Polytechnic.

I am currently a Diploma in Infocomm Security Management (DISM) student. So far, SP has been very educational and useful for broadening my horizons regarding my general knowledge about technology and security management. I have also been able to learn the very elementals of computers and the coding languages that allow us to communicate with them.

In this page I have included information for both full time and part time courses offered by Singapore Polytechnic and I have also given links that will take you directly to Singapore Polytechnic's website.
